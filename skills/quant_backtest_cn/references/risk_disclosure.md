# Risk Disclosure

Every result explanation must include risk limitations.

Required points:

- Backtest results do not represent future returns.
- Results depend on historical data quality and assumptions.
- Transaction costs and slippage are simplified.
- Liquidity, suspensions, limit-up/limit-down, and market impact may not be fully modeled.
- Parameter choices may be overfit.
- Short sample periods are not reliable.

Do not provide direct investment advice.

Good phrasing:

"这个结果只能说明该规则在当前历史数据和假设下的表现，不代表未来收益。建议继续看不同市场阶段、参数稳定性和交易成本敏感性。"
